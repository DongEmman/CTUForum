About CTU-DB Student Forum

Cebu Technological Universtiy Daanbantayan (CTU-DB) Student Forum is an online platform built to help students connect, share ideas, ask questions, and collaborate on academic topics in a safe and supportive space. This system promotes open discussion, learning, and peer support among Cebu Technological University – Daanbantayan Campus students. Here, you can post your questions, give answers, react to content, and grow your knowledge with your classmates.

"Empowering students to help each other — one question at a time."
